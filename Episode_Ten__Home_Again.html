In which a plan is made. Featuring: Orpheus returned from the underworld, a devastating phone call, cold comfort.

Episode description: Anna may be back, but is she really home? And what is that strange electrical buzzing?
==Episode Overview== Anna regroups, speaks to Mabel, and works out a new strategy.

Trigger Warnings

  • Distortion, death, mental issues, abuse, doomed thinking.

Episode Summary

Anna gets back to the House and finds Mabel able to communicate with her through the lights. They have a fight after Anna explains what happened to her; then Anna gets a phone call that Sally has passed away. The King comes in on Mabel's end and Anna tells Mabel a story about her nephew to distract her and keep her safe from the King. The episode ends with Anna asking Mabel to stay alive until she's able to get her out of the Underhill.
==Continuity== Please note that this section contains spoilers for later episodes of Mabel. Continue at your own risk.

  • Anna assumes the King is hurting Mabel in some way; rather ironically, Sally was the one who did that, and she's the one Anna is currently mourning.
  • Anna was afraid of Luna, originally; now that she's King, that's kind of out the window. In season seven, Mabel (and by extension Anna) are both intent on getting Luna back for what she's done.
