Episode description: Mabel tells a strange story, and eats a strange meal. 

Episode Overview

Mabel tells Anna how Luna and Sally met and subsequently destroyed each other. She also convinces The House to grow her a fruit tree.

Trigger Warnings

  • Unreality, distortion, loud noises, screaming, crying, misophonia triggering sounds.

Episode Summary

Mabel, trapped in the Martin house, discusses Luna and Sally's past. She explains that Luna, as the light which the dark needs to define itself, was always something that was wanted. Sally, who loved Luna but did not understand her, tried to save her before breaking her promise and leaving her to rot. Mabel discusses Sally's position of power and her sins against both Luna and Mabel herself.

The House grows Mabel a fruit tree to eat while she tells this story to Anna.
