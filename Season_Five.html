Metatextual Information

Season five took place in 2018, after a brief extension to the hiatus due to the writers of the show getting married.1 It returned on June 11th, 2018, and ended on July 9th, 2018. It introduced the character of Tressa Davies and took place in the Infinity Mirror timeline.

Non-Spoiler Plot

A listener of the podcast, Tressa Davies, wins a competition run by Becca De La Rosa and Maybell Marten to stay in Becca's grandmother's house, the place which inspired the Martin House in the show. Tressa stays in the house while recording herself answering questions the writers had given to her. Once she arrives, it rapidly becomes clear that the house, Becca, Maybell, and Tressa herself are not what they seem.
