Metatextual Information

Season two dropped between January 30th 2017 and March 20th 2017, with Letter From Juniper dropping as a pre-season bonus on January 1st, 2017. It is primarily narrated by Anna. Every episode save for Killing the Moon was written by Becca De La Rosa; Killing the Moon was written by Maybell Marten. It introduces the character of Monica Jenkins and names Thomas for the first time.

Non-Spoiler Plot Summary

After Anna's less-than-triumphant return to the "real" world, she attempts to figure out a way to get Mabel back to her.
