Background

Lang Primary Conservatory for the Disabled was a "cult school" ran by Aurora Silver. It was active sometime in the early 2000's. Aurora Silver, under the alias "Lara Ouvriers", manipulated the children in her care there, sorting them into classes based on their disability. After Tressa Davies tricked a girl into giving her a ring, Aurora Silver started having one-on-one sessions with Tressa, eventually ending in Tressa witnessing a ritual in which Aurora Silver sacrificed a boy codenamed the Mourning Dove by slitting his throat.
