Episode Overview

Mabel makes a reappearance.

Trigger Warnings

  • Blood, loud noise, unreality, depersonalization, disassociation.

Episode Summary

We meet back up with Mabel, who continuously tells Anna to leave her alone. She talks about not being real as a child (and not having green eyes, lending to the theory that she's a changeling) before talking about Luna Thorne, revealing her to be the girl in the mirror that she had mentioned in episode seven.
