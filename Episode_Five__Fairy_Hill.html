Episode Summary

Anna walks around the outside of the house and tells Mabel about various things-- her childhood, her desire to quit due to how strange the Martin house is getting but her inability to do so, and the signs she found outside.; a fairy ring of mushrooms and, most alarmingly, a sign calling the land Fairy Hill.
