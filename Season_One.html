Metatextual Information

Season one dropped between September 30th 2016 and November 21st 2016. It was largely written by Becca De La Rosa; only King in the Labyrinth was written by Maybell Marten. It introduces the characters Anna Limón, Mabel Martin, The House, Sally Martin, Lily Martin, Thomas, Jack Madrigal, Luna Thorne, and the King.

Non-Spoiler Plot Summary

A home nurse named Anna attempts to get into contact with the missing granddaughter of the woman she takes care of as new mysteries begin to unravel in the house she's found herself in.
